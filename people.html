Lab director

Hadar Fisher, PhD

Principal Investigator

Dr. Fisher is a clinical psychologist and an incoming Assistant Professor in the Department of Psychology at Bar-Ilan University. She received her PhD from Bar-Ilan University, completed her first postdoctoral fellowship at the University of Haifa as part of the Data Science Research Center, and her second at Harvard Medical School and McLean Hospital.

Her research examines the role of emotions in the etiology and treatment of depression. Her interests include affect dynamics and emotion regulation in daily life, emotional processes and mechanisms of change in psychotherapy, adolescent depression, and computational approaches to mental health, including natural language processing, large language models (LLMs), machine learning, facial expression analysis, and passive smartphone sensing. Her work has appeared in Nature, Nature Reviews Psychology, npj Digital Medicine, Emotion, Clinical Psychological Science, and Behaviour Research and Therapy, among others.
